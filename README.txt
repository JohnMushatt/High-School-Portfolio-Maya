Autodesk Maya 2014
1. Plug your USB drive into your computer.
2. Click Start > My Computer > double click your USB drive to open it.
3. On your camp USB drive, you'll find your project scene or if you created a render a Quicktime or AVI movie of your animation along with your project folder with all of the Maya files in it
4. To play your video, simply double-click it to open it up and run it on your computer
5. To continue working on your project in Maya at home, install and activate Maya 2014 on your computer.
6. Simply open Maya and set the project to the project folder you brought home with you.
7. Then open the latest scene file to continue
